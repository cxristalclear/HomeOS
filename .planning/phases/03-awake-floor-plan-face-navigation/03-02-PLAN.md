Build the awake floor-plan render and wire the minimal tap-to-wake state into `/wall` so tapping the ambient face flips to the awake face showing the correct Floor — Room tiles with amber Attention badges or clear-checks, the pinned Errands tile, the teal "Start here" flag + pre-selection on the wake room, and tap-to-select on any tile.
